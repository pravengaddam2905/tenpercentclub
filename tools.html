How This Calculation Works

Needs-Based Analysis Approach: This calculator uses the needs-based method to determine your life insurance requirements. It considers:

  • Immediate Needs: Debts that would need to be paid off immediately (mortgage, loans, funeral costs)
  • Income Replacement: Years of income your family would need, minus any survivor benefits
  • Future Goals: Major expenses like education or wedding costs
  • Existing Resources: Savings and current insurance that reduce the coverage gap

Assumptions:

  • Income replacement is calculated as a lump sum, not accounting for investment returns
  • If inflation adjustment is selected, we use compound inflation over the replacement period
  • CPP survivor benefits average $7,500/year (varies based on contributions)
  • This is a guideline estimate - actual needs may vary based on your specific situation

Important: This calculator provides an estimate only. For a comprehensive life insurance needs analysis tailored to your family's unique situation, book a free consultation with us.
